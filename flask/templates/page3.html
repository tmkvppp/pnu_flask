Jingle Ale - Spiced Ale

Jingle Ale is a delightful spiced ale, perfect for the holiday season. It features a blend of spices, giving it a warm and festive flavor. With an alcohol by volume (ABV) of 5.0%, it's a great choice for those who enjoy a flavorful and moderate beer.

Frosty Lager - Winter Lager

Frosty Lager is a winter favorite, known for its crisp and refreshing taste. With a 4.8% ABV, it's a light lager that can be enjoyed during the colder months. Its clean finish and subtle maltiness make it a versatile choice for various occasions.

Merry Stout - Chocolate Stout

Merry Stout is a rich and indulgent chocolate stout that satisfies the sweet tooth. At 6.2% ABV, it's a bold choice with deep chocolate flavors and a velvety smooth texture. Ideal for those who appreciate a decadent beer experience.
